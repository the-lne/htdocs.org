    Multiple downloads are hosted/mirrored here. Either clicking a file's respective download button or 'curl -LO htdocs.org/downloads/filename.extension' are sufficient ways for downloading.

    randomMovement.py and slightlysmarterbot.py are python bots who use the win32api and pynput modules to simulate userinput in slightly different ways. randomMovement uses pseudorandom integers, and slightlysmarterbot compares the floating point integer representations of colors from specific coordinants on the screen between two screenshots to decided when to submit pseudorandomized input.
